Hayden Gallery Show

I'm having a show this summer at the Hayden City Gallery for my watercolors! I'm so excited, this is going to be great! So, make sure you swing by the Hayden City Hall where my work will be hanging for three months from July through September.

My watercolors are wildly creative, unique works with a whimsical style that is all mine. Some are plein air and others are memories, but all tell marvelous stories.

People of the Palouse

This is my first gallery show since graduating from the University of Idaho and I'm excited for it to be in my home town in Hayden. It's been wonderful getting to know a few people who work for the city of Hayden and learning more about the gallery. I learned about the opportunity because I was looking for more ways to get involved in art making in the area. I thought that the city hall might have information about arts in the area. I was pleasantly surprised to find out about their own gallery. Thank you Hayden City Hall for giving me this opportunity to showcase some of my work! I'm looking forward to the show and hope many others are too.
