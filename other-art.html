Illustrations/Digital

 

Illustration was what got me into art. I've always loved drawing and I like to make people laugh with the drawings I make if I can. Most of these illustrations are from working for RAWR (a section of University of Idaho's student news paper) doing illustrations for them. However, I was drawing the Hood Comics before they asked me to start making them for the paper.
