By investigating the world through making, I understand the world better. Through creating works that allow others to experience the world in a new way I hope to share some of my investigation with others. Through exploration in my work I discover things that otherwise go unnoticed. Much of what I do is about slowing down, seeing my surroundings and responding to it through making.
