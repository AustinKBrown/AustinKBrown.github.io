Et Dices Ad Oderandum

AAN Gallery Installation

 

Et Dices Ad Oderandum translates from Latin to "Smell and Tell."

 

This work was inspired by a psychology lecture about how smells are the most likely sense to trigger memories. Masking tape webs held up glass jars filled with various smells. In this case I was relying on my community to help make the most significant part of the art. Comments on the smells and the memories they triggered created a rich and wonderful experiential piece of art. After
