Studio Adventures

While getting my art work all together and framed for the Moscow Renaissance fair I ran into this guy named Luciano. He's a singer and a guitarist and he was setting up his website. I asked him what he was up to and we got to talking. "I'm having a show at the Kenworthy." He told me. "Maybe you should put up some of your art work there during my recital."

Since that conversation things have really been picking up. I've been rushing to keep up and it's exciting. The buzz about the show is growing and I feel it quickly approaching.

This is going to be a great show. I'm very excited to be a part of it.

My body of work has been expanding and I'm looking forward to getting other people in Moscow looking at what I've been up to.

Whether you want to buy art or not, there's something to enjoy for everyone at an event like this. There will be music in Italian, German, and Russian. There will be wine. And of course there will be art. This is going to be a great time. I hope to you there.
