I'm looking forward to the Moscow Renaissance Fair. I'm going to have a booth this year on May 2-3.  I'll be selling paintings like these and others I've shown here.

I also found out that I've got a place in the Hayden City Gallery next year! I'm so excited! This will be my first gallery show outside of school! Summer 2016 is going to be good.

I might be getting published in some sort of art magazine in Europe too. I need to look into that a little more soon. An editor recently contacted me asking if I'd like to be featured. That would be pretty cool too. We'll see. I'll post more about that as I know more.

That's about it for updates for now. That food.... Nina's a really good cook. Mmmmm
